Whether I’m analyzing exam scheduling equity or helping underserved communities access better resources, my goal is always to create systems that work better for everyone.

Featured Projects

📊 Final Exam Scheduling Capstone

Led data analysis for UC Berkeley’s Registrar to uncover systemic inefficiencies in exam room usage. Identified 1,400+ underutilized rooms and delivered insights that influenced scheduling policy changes at the institutional level.

🏙️ Displacement & Education – SFBU Project

Contributed to a research initiative with the Latino Education Advancement Foundation exploring how displacement affects school engagement in communities of color. Work involved mixed-methods research and community-focused data analysis.

🌐 Political Risk: U.S.–China CPI & Tariffs

Created a Seasonal Anomaly Index using apparel CPI data to model the effects of 2025 U.S.–China tariffs. Applied time series modeling and communicated insights in the context of global trade and risk analysis.
